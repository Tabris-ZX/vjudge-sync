## Quick Start

### One-click AC Sync
**Synchronize with one click if you have linked your accounts on VJudge.**

1. After installing the script, visit the VJudge website; a side panel will appear.
2. Check the OJs you want to sync and click "One-click Sync".
3. To stop syncing, simply refresh the page.

![](panel.png)
![](popup.png)

### One-click Cookie Auto-fill
**Quickly fill cookies on the VJudge account settings page.**

1. Open the VJudge account binding page, and click the "Fill Cookie" button on the panel.
2. **Note**: This feature requires the browser extension version and does not support the "pinned window" mode.

![](auto-fill.png)
